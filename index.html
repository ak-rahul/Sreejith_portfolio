About Me

I am a media professional working across journalism, media research, and digital communications. Currently pursuing an MA in Journalism and Media Communications at the University of Hertfordshire, my work explores how media narratives, geopolitics, and digital platforms shape public understanding in today’s rapidly evolving information environment.

I began my career in journalism as an Intern Reporter at The New Indian Express, where I covered local events and wrote feature stories, gaining experience in reporting, interviewing, research, and fact-checking. This newsroom experience helped shape my approach to storytelling analytical, research-driven, and grounded in strong editorial standards.

Alongside journalism, I worked in digital media and communications, including public relations, content strategy, and social media management.

My work sits at the intersection of Journalism, Media Analysis, Digital Storytelling, Brand marketing & Advertising
